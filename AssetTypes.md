> specified. <br /><br />
> clear shell Clears the current shell. <br /><br />

> in your notecard) <br /><br />
> list guests Lists authorized guests (set
> in your notecard) <br /><br />

> hide shell Makes the shell invisible. <br /><br />
> hide floor Makes the shell's floor
> invisible (in supported shells.) <br /><br />

> show shell Makes the shell invisible. <br /><br />
> show floor Makes the shell's floor
> invisible (in supported shells.) <br /><br />

> to the control panel.      This arch is temporary and will vanish in about 30
> seconds or when the scene changes.<br /><br />
